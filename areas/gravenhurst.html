Muskoka Mortgage Expertise

Gravenhurst is the gateway to Muskoka—and Muskoka mortgages aren't like regular mortgages. Waterfront properties, seasonal access, unique construction types, and high-value cottages all require lenders who understand this market.

Not every lender is comfortable with cottage country. Some have restrictions on seasonal properties. Others don't understand water access or island properties. I know which lenders work well in Muskoka and how to present your application for the best chance of approval at the best rate.

Cottage & Waterfront Mortgages

Buying a cottage is different from buying a house in the city. There are additional considerations that can affect your financing:

  • Year-round vs. seasonal — Some lenders won't finance seasonal-only properties, or they require larger down payments
  • Water access — Boat-access properties can be more complex to finance
  • Property type — Older cottages, unique construction, or properties without full foundations may need specific lenders
  • Rental income — If you plan to rent your cottage, some lenders will consider that income
  • Higher values — Muskoka waterfront often means larger mortgages with different qualifying rules

I've helped clients finance everything from modest family cottages to multi-million dollar waterfront estates. Whatever your Muskoka dream looks like, let's talk about how to make it happen.

Refinancing Your Cottage

Already own in Muskoka? If your property has appreciated (and most have, significantly), you might be sitting on substantial equity. Refinancing can help you:

  • Fund renovations or upgrades to your cottage
  • Consolidate debt at a lower interest rate
  • Access capital for other investments
  • Help family members with their own down payments

Cottage refinancing can be more complex than residential, but I know which lenders offer the best options for Muskoka properties.

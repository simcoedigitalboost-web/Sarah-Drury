Why Use a Mortgage Broker?

Your bank works for themselves. I work for you. Here's why that matters.

The Difference Is Simple

When you walk into a bank, you get their products at their rates. When you work with me, you get access to 50+ lenders competing for your business. Same mortgage, different approach, potentially thousands of dollars in savings.

Going to Your Bank

  • Limited to one lender's products
  • Take it or leave it rate offers
  • Branch hours and wait times
  • Staff may rotate frequently
  • One-size-fits-all approach
  • May not see your full picture
  • Incentivized to sell bank products

Working With Me

  • Access to 50+ lenders
  • Lenders compete for your business
  • Available when you need me
  • One point of contact throughout
  • Solutions tailored to your situation
  • I see your complete financial picture
  • Incentivized to find you the best deal

The Real Benefits

Beyond just rate shopping, here's what working with a mortgage broker actually means for you.

01

Better Rates, Period

I have access to rates you won't find walking into a bank branch. Lenders offer brokers preferred pricing because we bring them volume. That savings goes directly to you.

02

One Application, Many Lenders

Instead of applying separately at multiple banks, you fill out one application with me. I shop it across my network and bring you the best options. Less paperwork, less hassle, better results.

03

Expert Navigation

Self-employed? Bruised credit? Unique property? I know which lenders specialize in different situations. Instead of getting declined and trying somewhere else, I send your file to the right place the first time.

04

Someone in Your Corner

Bank employees have sales targets for their products. I have one goal: finding you the best mortgage. My success depends on your satisfaction and referrals, so my interests are aligned with yours.

05

The Fine Print Matters

Two mortgages with the same rate can be very different products. I look at prepayment privileges, portability, penalties, and flexibility—the details that matter when life changes.

06

Usually Free to You

For most residential mortgages, my services cost you nothing. The lender pays my fee when your mortgage closes. You get expert help at no cost—that's a pretty good deal.

But Won't My Bank Match Any Offer?

Sometimes they will, sometimes they won't. But here's what most people don't realize: getting your bank to match a rate still leaves money on the table.

First, you have to do all the legwork yourself—getting quotes, comparing terms, going back and forth. That takes time and energy.

Second, rate is just one factor. Your bank might match the rate but not the terms. Things like prepayment options, penalty calculations, and flexibility can cost or save you thousands down the road.

Third, why negotiate alone when you can have an expert do it for you—for free? That's literally my job.

The Bottom Line

Your mortgage is probably the biggest financial commitment you'll ever make. It deserves more than a 15-minute conversation at your local branch. It deserves someone who will fight for the best deal, explain your options clearly, and guide you through the process from start to finish.

That's what I do. And I'd love to do it for you.

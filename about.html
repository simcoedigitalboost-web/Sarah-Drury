About Sarah

11 years of putting clients first. Here's what that actually means.

Not Your Typical Mortgage Broker

I got into this industry 11 years ago because I saw too many people getting bad advice—or no advice at all. They'd walk into their bank, take whatever rate was offered, and never realize they were leaving thousands of dollars on the table.

That didn't sit right with me.

So I became the person who fights on the other side. As a mortgage agent, I don't work for any single bank or lender. I work for you. My job is to shop your mortgage across dozens of lenders and find the best rate and terms for your specific situation.

Why I Do This

The best part of my job? Watching someone realize they just saved $15,000 over the life of their mortgage. Or helping a first-time buyer who thought they couldn't qualify finally get the keys to their home.

I'm also a mom of two, so I understand what's at stake. Your mortgage isn't just a financial product—it's your family's home. The place where your kids grow up. That's why I take this so seriously.

When I'm not working, you'll find me outside being active with my kids. I believe in balance, and I bring that same energy to how I work with clients: thorough but never overwhelming, professional but always human.

What Working With Me Looks Like

No jargon. No pressure. Just honest guidance.

We Have a Conversation

First, I listen. What are you trying to accomplish? What's your situation? What matters most to you? Every mortgage strategy starts with understanding your goals.

I Shop for You

With access to over 50 lenders, I compare rates and terms on your behalf. You get options you'd never see walking into a single bank—and I explain each one in plain English.

I Handle the Details

Paperwork, lender communication, deadlines—I manage it all. You stay informed without getting buried in the process. When you have questions, I'm a phone call away.

The Dominion Lending Difference

I'm proudly licensed with Dominion Lending Centres Elite Mortgage Group.

Dominion Lending Centres is one of Canada's largest mortgage networks, which means I have access to an extensive roster of lenders—from major banks to credit unions to alternative lenders. This isn't about pushing one bank's products. It's about finding the right fit for you.

Being part of DLC also means I'm backed by industry-leading support, compliance, and ongoing education. The mortgage world changes constantly—new rules, new programs, new opportunities. I stay current so you don't have to.
